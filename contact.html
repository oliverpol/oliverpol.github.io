Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together

What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in biomedical data analysis, statistical modeling, clinical trial data, omics data analysis, and creating reproducible research frameworks. I also work on data visualization projects and provide statistical consulting.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Simple analyses might take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive research projects can span several months. I provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide. I'm fluent in both Spanish and English, and I'm experienced in collaborating with international research teams and organizations.

What software and tools do you use?

My primary tools include R, Python, MATLAB, SQL, Power BI, and various specialized statistical packages. I focus on using open-source tools whenever possible to ensure reproducibility and accessibility.
